About This Class

Welcome to our class portfolio! We are proud first-year students of the Associate in Computer Technology program at Naga College Foundation. As beginners in the world of technology, we are eager to learn, explore, and develop our skills in coding, web development, and computer systems. Our class is a group of motivated learners who support each other in building a strong foundation for our future careers.

Learning & Development

Throughout our first year, we are introduced to fundamental programming languages, web development tools, and practical projects that help us apply what we learn in class. Each of us gains hands-on experience designing websites, creating simple applications, and solving technical problems, while also learning to work as a team and communicate effectively.

Collaboration & Growth

Beyond our studies, we embrace collaboration, creativity, and curiosity. We participate in class projects, exercises, and workshops that encourage problem-solving, innovation, and personal growth. Our supportive environment helps us build confidence, develop good work habits, and prepare for the challenges of the IT industry.

Portfolio Showcase

This portfolio reflects our journey as first-year students in the Associate in Computer Technology program. It showcases our projects, skills, ambitions, and dedication to learning. Each section highlights our progress, creativity, and teamwork as we grow into capable and responsible professionals ready to contribute to the technology field.

Our Vision

In essence, our class represents a safe, nurturing, and inspiring environment where first-year students can explore their potential, learn new skills, and build a solid foundation for future success in the world of technology.
